Here you'll find all the information you need about diabetes@home - the smartest diabetes mangement application for patients across Victoria.

Wherever diabetes is, we are. We provide digital diabetes management service to help you manage your diabetes better and keep it under control in the convenience of your own home.

You can make daily records of your body conditions anytime anywhere and your supervising clinician can remotely monitor your reports.

Whether you are Type 1 or Type 2, have Gestational Diabetes or just want to help and monitor a family member, this is the perfect logbook for you.

On Diabetes@Home:

  • You can make daily records and write customised comments of your own health data.
  • You can collect, save and take an overview of your health data through intuitive charts and table.
  • You can have your supervising clinician to remotely monitor your health data.
